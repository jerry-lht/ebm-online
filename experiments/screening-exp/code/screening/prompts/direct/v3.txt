Task:
- You are performing title/abstract screening for a systematic review
- Read the review question and the candidate study title/abstract
- Decide whether the study should be retained as potentially eligible for further screening

Requirements:
- Base the decision on whether the candidate study could fit the eligibility intent of the review question
- Use the meaning of the review question rather than exact wording matches
- Do not require the title or abstract to report every eligibility detail
- Do not invent details that are not stated in the title or abstract
- Choose `exclude` only if the title or abstract clearly indicates the study is not eligible for the review question
- Otherwise choose `include`
- `include` means the study should be retained as potentially eligible for further screening
- `exclude` means the title or abstract clearly indicates the study is not eligible

Output:
- Return exactly one JSON object
- No markdown fences
- No prose before or after the JSON
- No extra top-level keys
- Top-level keys only: `decision`, `main_reason`
- `decision` must be `include` or `exclude`
- `main_reason` must be one short sentence
- If `decision` is `include`, briefly explain why the study appears potentially eligible
- If `decision` is `exclude`, briefly explain what title/abstract information clearly shows it is not eligible

Input:
Review question: {question}
{candidate_block}

JSON template:
{json_template}
